Meet who we are

This is the team, the staff, the teachers who teach.

We do this as passion, not as a job.

The two departments are Digital Technology, and Geography.

In Digital Technology we help teach students all the basic programming skills every aspiring developers should have and more. We teach them how to use computer programs like photoshop or visual studio code that would later help them in life if they pursue computer science.

In Geography we teach our students maps and countries and stuff. We give our students the chance to study the monitoring and analysis of weather, waves, tides, soils, sediments and rivers; cultural and economic practices.
